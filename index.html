Featured Work
MSc Dissertation · Financial Research
Revenue Forecasting & Break-Even Analysis
for Subscription-Based Fintech Startups
Nottingham Trent University · 2024–2025

This dissertation examined how subscription-based fintech companies can accurately predict future revenue and determine the point at which they become profitable. The research developed an original financial framework and forecasting model built around key drivers including customer acquisition rates, subscription pricing tiers, churn rates, and operating cost structures.

Using this model, the study analysed the break-even timeline for fintech startups — identifying the revenue thresholds required to cover fixed and variable costs. The findings highlighted how accurate financial modelling supports strategic decision-making, risk management, and investor confidence in early-stage fintech businesses.
